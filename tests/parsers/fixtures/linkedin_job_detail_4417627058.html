Fides Treasury Services is a leading provider of multibank connectivity, payments, and transaction communications. With over 100 years of experience, we connect thousands of corporates to more than 13,000 banks and payment providers worldwide, enabling cash and liquidity management for treasury and finance teams.


If you are interested in a career with a company with a strong global presence and a focus on innovation and customer satisfaction, then Fides is the right place for you. We are looking for a committed Technical Requirements Engineer who would like to strengthen our team and help realize our vision.


Tasks

The Technical Requirements Engineer is responsible for the structured collection, analysis, and specification of technical requirements from business and IT stakeholders. The goal is to establish a clear, complete, and implementable requirements foundation that serves as the basis for development, integration, and operations.


This role acts as a key interface between business, architecture, development, and operations, ensuring that requirements are efficiently captured, well understood, and sustainably implemented, especially in complex and heterogeneous IT environments (e.g., legacy systems, middleware, integrations).


Responsibilities and Key Tasks:



  • Capture and structure technical requirements from business units and IT stakeholders

  • Translate business needs into clear technical specifications

  • Analyze existing systems, processes, and data flows (including legacy environments)

  • Create and maintain clear and structured documentation (e.g., Confluence, Jira)

  • Define interfaces, data formats, and integration requirements

  • Collaborate closely with architecture, development, operations, and external partners

  • Ensure end-to-end traceability of requirements across the lifecycle

  • Support prioritization and evaluation of requirements

  • Identify dependencies, risks, and optimization opportunities

  • Facilitate workshops and stakeholder alignment sessions

  • Ensure quality, completeness, and consistency of requirements

  • Support testing, acceptance, and handover to operations


Requirements

Professional Requirements:



  • Degree in Computer Science, Business Informatics, or a comparable qualification

  • Several years of experience in requirements engineering within complex IT environments

  • Experience with integration and middleware technologies (e.g., messaging, APIs, file transfer)

  • Strong understanding of IT architectures, system landscapes, and data flows

  • Experience with tools such as Jira, Confluence, or similar platforms

  • Familiarity with agile and/or traditional project methodologies


Methodological Skills:



  • Strong analytical and structured working approach

  • Ability to present complex topics in a clear and understandable way

  • Experience in modeling processes and requirements

  • High quality standards and focus on sustainable solutions


Personal Skills:



  • Excellent communication and facilitation skills

  • Ability to bridge the gap between business and IT

  • High level of ownership and proactivity

  • Strong team player with assertiveness
